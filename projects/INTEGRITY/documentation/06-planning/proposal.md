## Executive Summary

### Project Overview

**Project INTEGRITY** is a strategic initiative to implement an AI-driven quality intelligence platform that automates test selection, identifies coverage gaps, predicts defects, and optimizes infrastructure costs.

**Status:** Approved for full development (PoC validation pending May 7)
