# Stakeholder Map: Project INTEGRITY

**Autonomous Quality Intelligence Ecosystem (AQIE)**

*Comprehensive Stakeholder Analysis and Engagement Strategy*

---

## Executive Summary

Project INTEGRITY affects multiple organizational stakeholders across technical, operational, and strategic dimensions. This stakeholder map identifies key influencers, documents their interests and concerns, and establishes targeted engagement strategies to ensure successful adoption of the Autonomous Quality Intelligence Ecosystem.

**Key Findings:**
- **12 Primary Stakeholders** identified across 4 stakeholder categories
- **Critical Success Factors:** Executive sponsorship + Engineering buy-in + Organizational change management
- **Risk Factors:** Change resistance + Integration complexity + Team reskilling requirements
- **Engagement Priority:** Immediate engagement with Executive Sponsor + Technical Leadership
