## Opportunity Assessment Framework

### Evaluation Criteria

This analysis employs a balanced scorecard approach, evaluating initiatives across five strategic dimensions:

1. **Strategic Criticality** (Weight: 25%) - Does this address existential competitive threats?
2. **Financial ROI** (Weight: 25%) - What is the cost/benefit ratio and payback period?

## Decision Criteria & Go/No-Go Thresholds

### PoC Success Criteria (7-Day Sprint)

The following metrics will determine proceed/stop decision:

| Criterion | Success Target | Validation Method |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Coverage Gap Identification** | ≥ 20% improvement opportunity | Gap analysis report |
| **Predictive Accuracy** | ≥ 85% in PoC scenario | Prediction vs. actual comparison |
| **Test Reduction Achievement** | ≥ 60% test count reduction | Pipeline metrics |
| **Resilience Validation** | ≥ 90% auto-recovery success | Chaos engineering results |
| **Integration Stability** | Zero critical integration failures | Incident log review |
| **Stakeholder Satisfaction** | ≥ 80% approving teams | Survey feedback |

### Go/No-Go Decision Logic

**PROCEED IF:**
- ✅ ≥ 5 of 6 success criteria met
- ✅ No critical blockers identified
- ✅ Executive sponsor confirms investment decision
- ✅ Resource plan finalized

**MODIFY IF:**
- ⚠️ 3-4 success criteria met, but with identified workarounds
- ⚠️ Implementation plan adjusted to address findings
- ⚠️ Timeline extended for additional risk mitigation

**STOP IF:**
- ❌ < 3 success criteria met
- ❌ Critical technical blocker identified (data privacy, privacy controls)
- ❌ Executive sponsor withdraws support
- ❌ Feasibility concerns exceed acceptable risk threshold
