### Primary Source Document

**Source:** project-definition.md (Business Case)  
**Date:** April 2026  
**Author:** Lau Hernández, Senior Software Quality & Automation Engineer  
**Classification:** Strategic

**Extracted Content:**

1. **Executive Summary:**
   - AQIE as paradigm shift from reactive to proactive testing
   - Focus on "software integrity" not "test quantity"
   - AI-driven observability and predictive analytics

2. **Problem Statement:**
   - Maintenance Burden: 35% engineering capacity lost
   - Release Latency: "run-all" regression bottleneck
   - Operational Blind Spots: lab vs. production disconnect
   - Infrastructure Costs: redundant test execution waste

3. **Triple-Pillar Framework:**
   - Pillar I: Observability-Driven Intelligence (AI agents + telemetry)
   - Pillar II: Predictive Impact Analytics (LLM-powered + 85% reduction)
   - Pillar III: Autonomous Resilience (Chaos Engineering + 99.9% availability)

4. **Strategic ROI:**
   - Feedback Loop: 4-6 hours → 12-15 minutes (95% gain)
   - Escaped Bug Rate: 5.5% → <0.7% (87% mitigation)
   - Maintenance Effort: 40% → <5% of sprint
   - Infrastructure Cost: 70% ROI

5. **Implementation Roadmap:**
   - 7-Day Sprint format
   - Days 1-2: Evidence (production log analysis)
   - Days 3-4: Integration (Predictive Engine deployment)
   - Day 5: Stress Test (Chaos event showcase)
   - Days 6-7: Insights (Dashboard + executive briefing)

6. **Strategic Standards:**
   - Privacy-First approach (local LLM support)
   - Fidelity Testing (120fps, haptic feedback, latency)
   - Software Integrity Score transparency
   - Apple-standard UX excellence
