## Presentation Strategy

### Audience Analysis

**Primary Audience:** Executive Leadership / Board
- **Care About:** ROI, competitive advantage, risk management
- **Attention Span:** 15-20 minutes presentation + 10 minutes Q&A
- **Decision Authority:** Budget approval, resource allocation

**Secondary Audience:** Project Sponsor (Lau Hernández)
- **Care About:** Delivery capability, team impact, innovation leadership
- **Role:** Project execution sponsor, technical validation

### Content Flow Strategy

1. **Open (Slides 1-2):** Problem statement + financial impact
   - *Objective:* Grab attention with "$4M opportunity" and "2.6-month payback"
   
2. **Innovate (Slides 3-4):** Strategic value + quantified outcomes
   - *Objective:* Build confidence in solution quality and differentiation

3. **Validate (Slides 5-7):** PoC plan + readiness + risk mitigations
   - *Objective:* Show we have a plan and can execute successfully

4. **Differentiate (Slide 8):** Competitive positioning
   - *Objective:* Emphasize market impact and strategic advantage

5. **Decide (Slides 9-11):** Clear decision framework + call to action
   - *Objective:* Make decision easy (clear criteria, low-risk PoC path)

6. **Recap (Slide 12):** One-page summary
   - *Objective:* Leave executives with clear takeaway

### Visual Presentation Notes

- **Color Scheme:** Professional (blues/greens for growth, not red for risk)
- **Metrics Emphasis:** Use large numbers (8.5/10 score, $4.12M savings, 466% ROI)
- **Risk Framing:** "Manageable" and "mitigated", not "concerning"
- **Timeline:** Emphasize "7-day PoC" (fast validation, not months of planning)
